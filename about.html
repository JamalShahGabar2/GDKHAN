About Dr. Ghulam Dastgir Khan

Dr. Ghulam Dastgir Khan is an Assistant Professor at the IDEC Institute, Hiroshima University. His research focuses on empirical development economics, particularly in areas such as extractive industries, agriculture, rural development, and migration. He employs causal inference methods to generate evidence-based insights aimed at informing effective and equitable development policies.
